Summer in Slovenia

Somewhere, Triglav National Park, Slovenia August 2016

Recently, and with the idea of bringing a bit of order in the chaos that lately rules our day to day lifes, I got to engage in the seemingly impossible task of organizing my pictures.

Notably, I have always made an effort to bring my camera with me to partially document my trips, and thus, a considerable amount of pictures have been accumulating without receiving any further attention, they have been abandoned. Bunches of pictures ignored despite the invested diligent effort.

With the intent of dusting off some of my old picture I’ll be selecting a couple and publishing them in this blog. To start with, here are some pictures of Slovenia taken some four years ago.
