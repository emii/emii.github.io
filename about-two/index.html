EMILIANO IZQUIERDO

*(is‧kjer‧do)

Hi _ My name is Emiliano Izquierdo*, currently a PhD fellow at EMBL. I am studying small living stuff –the one which is continously changing, varied in shape, beautiful… functionally superb. When I am not doing experiments in the lab, I am outside getting some fresh air and ideas.

I was born in Mexico City and rised in Guanajuato, learned about genomics in Cuernavaca, and tried my luck with yeast genetics in Irapuato. In 2011 I moved to Germany and apart from a breif stay in Utrecht (NL) I’ve been happily living in Heidelberg ever since.

Apart from coffee, my everyday recipe inclides biology, some coding, and a bit of any kind of sport activity.

I like simple things, like sandwiches and coffee (always nice opportunities to get to know people), I always squeeze up from the bottom on a tube of toothpaste, whatever that means.

I am unlucky in cards.

I tend to be verbose.

PROJECTS

sndbx.be is not science

I made sndbx.be just as an experiment, a sandbox to draft and release thoughts to the outside, to find a place for this voice in the densely populated cloud of ideas. A creative outlet and a personal dominion.

Behind the HTML/CSS, there are markdown archives processed by Pelican, a static blog generator written in Python. The typefaces are Quicksand and Open Sans provided by Google fonts API.
